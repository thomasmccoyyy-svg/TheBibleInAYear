About the Series

A Daily Walk Through
God's Living Word

The Bible in a Year is a guided journey through all 66 books of the Bible. Each episode unpacks the day's readings with thoughtful commentary, historical context, and practical reflections to deepen your understanding of Scripture.

Whether you've read the Bible many times or are opening it for the first time, this series meets you where you are and walks with you — one day at a time.
